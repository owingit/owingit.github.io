om - research

I employ computer vision, agent-based modeling, network science, and artificial intelligence to study animal behavior and ecology. I am a Ph.D candidate at the University of Colorado Boulder and I work in the Peleg Lab and with Xerces Society for Invertebrate Conservation. Currently, my main research focus is the behavior of Lampyridae beetles, or fireflies.
