om - art

a study of birds

I paint birds whenever I get the chance. I am fascinated by the intelligence, natural beauty, and ecological savvy of the world's birds and I frequently wander the countryside, armed with binoculars and my ears, looking for feathered friends. use the left and right arrow keys to navigate the galleries below.
